Cheese Academy

About

Let's make a cheese that will change the world.

The Cheese Academy is a cheesemaker training school.

We want as many people as possible to know about the wonderfulness of cheese through self-sufficiency.

With this in mind, Cheese Academy TOKYO, a school for training cheese makers, has begun its journey.

After graduation, we will not only back up your self-sufficiency in cheese, but also help you find a job or change careers to become a cheesemaker.
